WTF is Creativity!?
The C stands for

Comfort zone? GET OUT OF THERE!
The R stands for

Results, don't be afraid to show them!
The E stands for

Express yourself with the things you make.
The A stands for

An Artist, that's what you are!
The T stands for

Think outside of the box!
The best ideas start from the dumbest of things
such as: a sock, a cardboard box or even a waterfall
The I stands for

I. Am. Me. My work, My ideas.. MINE!
The V stands for

Variety, don't stick to a single style!
The (other) I stands for

Improve. Learn from your old work, dig up new skills
and show the world how much you've grown!
The second T stands for

Tackle obstacles! You don't need anything in your way.

Keep going, no matter the ups and downs!
The Y stands for

Years, you will never learn something in a few days.
Take your time!
Express yourself and inspire others
